Shivrajya Pratishthan

New Anand park A1/A2/A3

Our Killa is proudly created by the talented kids of New Anand Park. Every year, we come together with boundless energy and creativity to rebuild the legacy of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj through our handmade fort. From collecting materials to painting every wall with care, we pour our hearts into this project.
